## Copy, cut and paste
NORMAL mode

- Copying/cutting something is called _yanking_. So the shortcut to _yank_ is `y`
- `yy` will copy the entire line
    - first `y` indicates the start of the yank operation
    - second `y` specifies the scope of the yank operation
- `p` (lowercase) will paste on the line below
- `P` (uppercase) will paste on the line above
- `dd` will yank and delete, basically what you would call _cut_ in a traditional editor
